welcome. we're tvirtas. we host free services for the public. we're based in the eastern US, but we think the name fits the purpose of this project. tvirtas (TVEER-tuhs) is lithuanian for "solid", but depending on how it's interpreted, it can also mean "stable", or "trustworthy." privacy and stability should be an expectation, not a privilege. the internet should be free of mass surveillance and data collection. it is currently not, obviously. we look to play a part in changing that, even if said part is small and irrelevant to some. services (more later): - matrix - subdomains - email other: - contact us/donate - rules/reminders
